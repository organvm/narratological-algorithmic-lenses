EL’s (16) shoulder-length, pin-straight, black, and wet hair, zip to the other end of her shoulder as her head swings toward the door.

Her full body contorts as she screams without making a sound.

She grabs her phone from the counter, lowers the music a few notches--discovers the source of the notification from before.

A confused eyebrow-lift dives into a smile. 

With the music back to its original volume, El dances in the bathroom, loading her toothbrush with paste, and brushes her teeth with vigor.

Her eyes jump up and down within the mirror, the pupils grow larger and larger until they take up her whole eye and the black spills down her cheeks.

She dabs the running black off of her face, and gives a smile and thumbs up to the mirror.
