INT. SANDRA’S APARTMENT - LIVING ROOM - MORNING #1#

EL (14), a scrappy teen, slouches on the right-most cushion of the three cushioned couch, her cellphone ready for use in her hands.  An occasional squirm or twitch works its way through her. 

Behind her the morning sun fights through the fast moving clouds and air conditioning disturbed blinds. 

Vines thick and thin crawl out from underneath the couch, and wrap their tendrils around El’s ankles, working their way up inch by inch.

@SANDRA (O.S.)
Dave got fired.

@DAVE (O.S.)
Dave got let go.

Across from El, on two almost touching love seats sit two almost touching adults, SANDRA (40s) and DAVE (40s). Sandra dons formal wear; and Dave...well, Dave is still in his pajamas.

Sandra shoots Dave a look of disapproval. A very very very...very tired look. Very tired.

@DAVE
There’s a difference.

@SANDRA

@DAVE
Severance?

@SANDRA

Dave answers with no answer and a sip from a mug of way too hot coffee.

@SANDRA

The vines wrap around her calfs, then clutch her knees. She tries to fight them off, but that just aggravates them further. 

@DAVE (O.S.)

@SANDRA (O.S.)
No--

Dave can’t make eye contact with El.

@DAVE

(Getting choked up)

@SANDRA

@DAVE

Sandra leans a bit closer to bridge the gap.

@SANDRA

*Gulp...*

Sandra procures another similar stack, dropping it right near her chair. Sandra sits and readjusts herself, taking a breath.

The vines secure El’s waist to the couch, and crawl further up toward her chest.

@DAVE (O.S.)

@SANDRA (O.S.)

@DAVE (O.S.)
Condition.

@SANDRA (O.S.)
Condition. Right.

Sandra and Dave, mirroring each other, push bifocals up the bridges of their noses, and collectively center themselves before--

Opening the folders, papers zip out whacking the ceiling and spilling out on the floor. The papers swarm around their heads, little dive bombers on suicide missions. The apartment begins to fill and threatens to swallow them all whole.

Sandra and Dave frantically sort through the papers, grabbing at them, then throwing them free, trying to find something--

Anything!

@SANDRA

@DAVE

@DAVE

@SANDRA (O.S.)

@DAVE (O.S.)

@SANDRA (O.S.)
